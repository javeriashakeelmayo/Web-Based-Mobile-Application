Common Questions

What are your opening hours? ▼

We serve daily from 12:00 PM to 01:00 AM. Last dine-in and delivery orders are taken 30 minutes before closing.

Where are you located? ▼

Find us at Plot 14-C, Stadium Commercial Lane 3, Block A, Dubai Chowk Area, Bahawalpur, Pakistan (Next to Gourmet Bakers).

Is the food served 100% Halal? ▼

Yes, absolutely. All our premium beef patties, raw chicken, and condiments are sourced strictly from ISO and Halal-certified corporate suppliers.

What are your delivery areas and charges? ▼

Home delivery is entirely FREE within a 5km radius of Dubai Chowk. For outer destinations up to 12km, a flat Rs. 150 rider fuel charge applies.
