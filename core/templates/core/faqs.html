Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about dining, delivery, and reservations at Atlas.

Is the food served at Atlas Dining 100% Halal?

Yes, absolutely. All our meats, ingredients, and spices are sourced exclusively from ISO and Halal-certified suppliers in Pakistan. Quality assurance is our highest priority.

What are your delivery areas and charges?

We provide free delivery within a 5km radius of Dubai Chowk, Bahawalpur. For locations further away (up to 12km), a nominal flat rider fuel fee of Rs. 150 applies.

Can I cancel or modify my table reservation?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your table reservation up to 45 minutes before your scheduled slot directly via our phone lines or support WhatsApp without any fee.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), Direct Cash at the counter, Visa/Mastercard credit/debit cards, as well as digital wallets including JazzCash, EasyPaisa, and Nayapay transfers.

Do you have vegan, vegetarian, or gluten-free options?

While our specialties lie in premium meats, our starters (like Truffle Fries) and designated mocktails are entirely vegetarian. You can request our chefs to make adjustments on custom requirements.
